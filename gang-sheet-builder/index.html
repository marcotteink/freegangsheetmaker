What is a gang sheet builder?

A gang sheet builder is an online tool that arranges many separate designs onto one sheet of DTF (direct-to-film) transfer material. Instead of printing each design on its own strip of film, you “gang” dozens of designs onto a single roll-width sheet. Because DTF is priced by the length of film used, packing your designs tightly is the easiest way to lower your cost per transfer. A good builder does the packing math for you so you do not have to nudge artwork around by hand in Photoshop or Illustrator.

How this free gang sheet builder works

Free Gang Sheet Maker is a browser-based builder designed around one goal: the tightest layout with the least wasted film. Here is what happens when you use it:

  • Upload: drag and drop your transparent PNG designs. Each file is measured automatically.
  • Set quantities and size: choose how many of each design you need and resize any design in inches, with a live DPI quality rating so nothing prints blurry.
  • Pick roll width and gap: select your film width (22 inches is standard) and the spacing between designs.
  • Pack: a maximum-rectangles bin-packing algorithm arranges everything — including duplicates — into the shortest possible sheet, rotating designs when it produces a tighter fit.
  • Export: download a print-ready 300 DPI PNG. Long sheets are split into multiple pages and bundled into a ZIP.
