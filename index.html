How to make a DTF gang sheet for free

A gang sheet packs many separate designs onto one DTF transfer film so you use less material and pay less per print. This free gang sheet maker does the packing math for you. Upload your transparent PNG designs, set the quantity of each one, choose your roll width, and press Render Layout. The tool arranges every design into the shortest possible sheet, then exports a print-ready PNG at 300 DPI. Sheets longer than your chosen page length are split into multiple pages and bundled into a ZIP automatically.

1. Export transparent PNGs at print size

Save each design as a PNG with a transparent background at the real size you want it printed, at 300 DPI. The tool reads the pixel dimensions of each file so your prints come out at true size.

2. Set quantities and trim

Use the quantity control to print several copies of the same design, and turn on Trim to crop away empty transparent borders so designs pack tighter. Leave rotation on to let the engine turn designs when it produces a tighter fit.

3. Render, check the stats, download

The stats show how many designs were placed, the final sheet size, how much film is actually covered, and how many pages were produced. When you are happy, download the print-ready file and send it to your printer, or order finished transfers from DTF.STREAM.

Frequently asked questions

Is this gang sheet maker really free?

Yes. It is completely free with no account, no watermark, and no limits. Everything runs in your browser, so your artwork is never uploaded to a server.

What is a DTF gang sheet?

A DTF gang sheet is one sheet of direct-to-film transfer material holding many designs packed together. Ganging designs onto a single sheet uses less film and lowers your cost per print.

What file type and resolution should I use?

Transparent PNG files exported at 300 DPI at the real print size. The tool lays everything out at 300 DPI by default so designs print at true size.

How does it save me money?

The packing engine arranges every design, including duplicates, into the smallest sheet length possible. Less used film per sheet means a lower material cost on every order.

Are my designs private?

Yes. The tool never sends your files anywhere. All measuring, packing, and rendering happens locally in your browser.
