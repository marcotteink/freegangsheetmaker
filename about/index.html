About Matt Marcotte

Free Gang Sheet Maker was built by Matt Marcotte, founder of DTF.STREAM and ScreenPrintGPT.

The maker

Matt Marcotte builds software and tools for the direct-to-film (DTF) and screen-printing industry. He is the founder of DTF.STREAM, a DTF transfer printing service, and ScreenPrintGPT, an AI assistant for print shops.

Why this tool is free

Most gang sheet builders are locked to a single print shop's checkout. Matt built Free Gang Sheet Maker as an independent, free tool so any printer or small apparel business can gang their designs, minimize wasted film, and export a print-ready file to use anywhere — no account, no fees, and no artwork ever uploaded to a server. It is a genuinely useful tool first, built on real experience running DTF and screen-print operations.
